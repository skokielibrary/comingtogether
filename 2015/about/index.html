Coming Together in Skokie & Niles Township tries to build knowlege of and appreciation of the diversity represented in Skokie and Niles Township. This year, Coming Together provides opportunities to explore race through reading selected books, discussing the myriad topics involved with race and racism, and working together to build a stronger community.
