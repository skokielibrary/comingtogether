About

Coming Together seeks to build knowledge of and appreciation for the diversity represented in Skokie and Niles Township. This year’s theme, Celebrating Muslim American CultureS, provides opportunities to explore Muslim American identity and culture through reading selected texts and engaging programming for all ages.

Get involved
