IL Dream Fund

Requirements

  • Must have at least one parents who immigrated to the U.S.
  • Lived with a parent or guardian while going to high school in Illinois.
  • Graduated from an Illinois high school or received a GED.
  • Must have attended school in Illinois for at least 3 years.
  • Current high school seniors are eligible to apply.
  • The HB 60 In-State Tuition law requires undocumented students to sign an affidavit promising to legalize his or her immigration status as soon as eligible.

Anhelo Project

Online Application
Application due date: January 15, 2016

Requirements

  • Two letters of recommendation.
  • One from advisor, counselor, or professor stating your academic success.
  • One from advisor, professor, college administrator, or community organization stating how your undocumented status has impacted your ineligibility for federal assistance and financial hardships in the 2015-2016 academic year.

Contact

  • www.theanheloproject.org
  • dreamscholoarshipchicago@gmail.com

Becas Univision

Online Application

  • Consideration I: January 31
  • Consideration II: February 28
  • General Deadline: March 30

Requirements

  • Minimum of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Be of Hispanic heritage.
  • Must be 1st generation to attend college.
  • Demonstrate financial need. This is determined based on your family income, number of people that reside in your household and the number of college students in your home.

Contact

  • univision.hsfts.net

CORE Que Llueva Cafe

Online Application
Due Date: February 29

Requirements:

  • Must be an undocumented student of Chicano/Latino descent.
  • Applicant must be graduating from high school or receiving their GED and enrolling for the first time in an accredited college or university in the United States or Puerto Rico.
  • Applicants must demonstrate financial need and academic potential and promise.
